Keep doing a static website simple

Wow! I am impressed. I was wondering the best way of generating a static website. I searched a lot. A big bunch of tools exist for sure it depends of your needs. And not everybody can spend too much time on it... like me.

Making a website can be hellish. Choose a provider, reserve a name for your site, choose a tool to design your pages, find out the best CSS and so on.

Myself I choose these instruments :

  • Emacs
  • Org-page
  • Github.io

and that's it!
